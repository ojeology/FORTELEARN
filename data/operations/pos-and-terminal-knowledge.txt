F2 BUTTON üîò ( PAYOUT/CANCEL TICKETS)..
This is used to pay out winning tickets and cancel both prematch and virtual tickets..
the operator can payout/cancel a ticket by either scanning the barcode on the ticket or entering the code manually.
Once he enters the code and clicks on enters the operator then proceeds to click on the space bar then a little confirmation message popups to show that the ticket has been paid out/cancel successfully.

A prematch ticked can only be cancelled on the same terminal it's been played within ten minutes of placing the bet,after 10 minutes it can no longer be cancelled.
And even within that ten minutes if any events starts the ticket can no longer be cancelled..
A virtual ticket can be cancelled anytime as long as none of the events on the ticket has started..

A prematch ticket can be cancelled in two ways.
Scan and cancel
Enter the code manually on f2
A virtual ticket can be cancelled in three ways
Scan and cancel 
Enter code manually 
Cancel last..

Paid out tickets and cancelled tickets are expected to be trashed and not returned to customers..
