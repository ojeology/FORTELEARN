Welcome bonus is a bonus given to newly registered customers of fortebet.
It is 3x their total deposit or 300 percent of their account balance at the moment of placing their first eligible bet.
A customer first eligible bet is either a prematch game or I play(live) game...
Once this is met the customers are given 3x of their deposits which is stored inside their fortebet welcome bonus account which is not redeemable yet.
For a customer to redeem this bonus, the customers needs to play a prematch or I play game with a minimum of three selections and total odds of 3.00 or more,
Whenever a customer does this (2) percent of the customers stake is deducted from the customer welcome bonus balance and credited to the customer main account balance which the customer can withdraw, or used to place bets...
NOTE the maximum a customer can get on his welcome bonus account is 1million naira.
meaning no matter the amount the customer deposit he will not be getting more than 1million naira.
Lastly welcome bonus lasts for 90days starting from the day the customer made his first deposit...
